Three Engineering Pillars

Privacy, Craft, Compliance

Every primecodevaultlink release is shaped by three non-negotiable engineering pillars. None is optional; none is a marketing line. They're how we sleep at night and how our partners ship confidently.

Privacy-First Architecture

All personal data lives on the user's device, encrypted at rest with AES-256 and only leaves the device when the user explicitly authorises a backup. No cloud profiling, no cross-app tracking, no behaviour sold to third parties.

  • Local-first storage by default
  • End-to-end encrypted user backups
  • Zero data sales, ever
  • Transparent privacy labels

Programmatic Craft

In-house procedural audio synthesis, organic motion design and adaptive UI generation. We treat every interaction curve, every animation easing, every typeface as a deliberate choice — never a default.

  • Procedural audio synthesis engine
  • Hand-tuned motion language
  • Adaptive UI generation
  • Custom typography & iconography

Global Compliance Doctrine

Every release is audited against GDPR, UK-GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, VCDPA, LGPD, PIPL, DPDP Act, and the EU Digital Services Act. Quarterly reviews catch drift before the regulators do.

  • GDPR / CCPA / LGPD / PIPL
  • DSA transparency reports
  • Quarterly compliance audits
  • Per-region consent orchestration

Our Process

From Brief To App Store

Six clear stages, each with a named owner, weekly demo, and a written acceptance checklist. No surprises at submission.

Week 1 — Discovery

Discovery & Compliance Brief

We co-author the privacy posture, define the data inventory, lock the regional scope, and produce a written compliance matrix covering GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, DSA, PIPL, and the App Store / Google Play submission constraints.

Week 2-3 — Design

Design Language & Prototype

Motion study, typography, colour tokens, icon system, and an interactive Figma prototype that exercises the full user journey. We deliberately ship two variants before we ship one.

Week 4-8 — Engineering

Engineering & SDK Integration

Swift / Kotlin native with shared design system. Ad mediation, ATT orchestration, StoreKit / Play Billing and CMP are wired in with feature flags and per-region gating from day one.

Week 9-10 — Compliance Audit

Pre-Submission Compliance Audit

Privacy labels, data-safety form, age rating, advertising disclosure, consent flow walkthrough, ATT text review, and a full read-through of the legal pages against the technical implementation.

Week 11-12 — Submission

App Store & Google Play Submission

Metadata, screenshots in 14 languages, review-response playbook, and the actual submission. We handle every review note until the build is live in every store.

Ongoing — Lifecycle

Lifecycle, Security & Compliance Reviews

Quarterly compliance reviews, monthly SDK upgrade audits, security patching, and a quarterly roadmap session. We are the long-term engineering partner, not the throw-it-over-the-wall vendor.
