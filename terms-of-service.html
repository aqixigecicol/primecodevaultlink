4. IAA Fraud — Detailed Penalty Schedule

"IAA fraud" is any conduct designed to extract rewards or otherwise exploit the advertising system in violation of these Terms. It is detected through a combination of device fingerprint, behaviour pattern, in-app telemetry, and partner-side signals (AppsFlyer / Adjust / Singular). The penalties below apply cumulatively and may be combined with legal action.

4.1 Examples of IAA Fraud

  • Using plugins, scripts, mods, or "auto-tap" tools to skip, accelerate, or simulate ad viewing.
  • Operating multiple accounts or multiple devices to mass-watch ads and resell the rewards.
  • Modifying device parameters (device ID, IMEI, MAC, IP) or using VPN / proxy to bypass regional price / reward rules.
  • Frequently switching apps, locking, or rebooting the device during ad playback to bypass the "full watch" requirement.
  • Exploiting an ad-platform bug to fabricate ad-impression or ad-click events.
  • Any other conduct that interferes with normal ad delivery, ad measurement, or reward accounting.

4.2 Penalty Schedule

OccurrencePenalty
First violation Warning; forfeit of all unredeemed ad-derived rewards in the current account balance; ad-viewing privileges suspended for 7 days.
Second violation Ad-viewing privileges suspended for 30 days; all ad-derived rewards in the account forfeited; device fingerprint recorded in our internal anti-fraud ledger.
Third or subsequent violation Permanent revocation of ad-viewing privileges on the account; the relevant device is blocked from accessing the App; the account is added to our internal anti-fraud black-list; we reserve the right to seek civil damages for lost ad revenue and to refer the matter to law-enforcement authorities.
Severe cases (mass fraud, organised abuse, deliberate exploitation of attribution systems) All of the above, plus immediate permanent account termination, immediate permanent device ban, notification to the App Store / Google Play, and pursuit of civil damages. Where criminal conduct is suspected (e.g. fraudulent chargebacks, identity fraud, money laundering), the matter is referred to law-enforcement authorities.

The anti-fraud system may take automatic action before the user is contacted. Where an automated decision materially affects you, you may request human review by writing to support@primecodevaultlink.com with the subject "Anti-Fraud Review".

6. IAP Fraud — Detailed Penalty Schedule

"IAP fraud" is any conduct designed to obtain virtual items, premium features, or refunds through dishonest means. The penalties below apply cumulatively and may be combined with civil and / or criminal action.

6.1 Examples of IAP Fraud

  • Malicious refund abuse: purchasing a virtual item, using it, then requesting a refund without returning the used item — for example, spending premium currency, then claiming a refund.
  • Payment-process tampering: using plugins, scripts, modified (cracked) Apps, or any technique to bypass the official payment channel.
  • Forged records: fabricating an order, a payment receipt, or a refund document to obtain virtual items or refunds.
  • Stolen payment methods: using a stolen bank card, stolen payment account, or unlawfully obtained redemption code.
  • Regional price arbitrage: using VPN / proxy to mask the user's region and purchase in a lower-priced region.
  • New-user bonus abuse: mass-registering accounts to exploit new-user promotions, trial bonuses, or first-purchase discounts, then re-selling the obtained virtual items.
  • Any other conduct intended to evade payment or to obtain virtual items / refunds dishonestly.

6.2 Penalty Schedule

OccurrencePenalty
First violation Warning; revocation of all fraudulently obtained virtual items; IAP privileges suspended for 15 days.
Second violation IAP privileges suspended for 90 days; all virtual items in the account revoked; device fingerprint recorded; the account is added to the internal anti-fraud black-list.
Third or subsequent violation Permanent revocation of IAP privileges; permanent device ban; permanent account termination; we report the matter to Apple / Google and cooperate with their enforcement actions; we reserve the right to seek civil damages.
Severe cases (mass fraud, organised abuse, stolen payment methods, money-laundering suspicion) All of the above, immediate permanent account termination, immediate permanent device ban, reporting to Apple / Google and to the relevant payment network, civil damages claim, and referral to law-enforcement authorities.
